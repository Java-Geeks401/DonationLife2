What We Do

Our website allows people to donate to poor people and Charity organizations by classifying donation into groups so that it is easier for the poor people to get what they want and verify that the donations match the needs of the poor people and allows people to select the charity they want to donate to.

Our Vision

We envision a future in which all people can pursue the opportunities they choose, raise healthier families, and can live normal life .

Our Mission

The Donation-Equal-Life mission is to improve the living situation of the poor, provide them with their basic needs and have their right to live in a dignified life.

Our History

Donation-Equal-Life We started from September 2021 with 5 programmers and we aspire to increase the number of the team to include customer service so that it is easy to communicate with us to increase project ideas

Social Work

Through our application, we work to connect donors to charity organizations, which in turn seek to meet the needs of the poor and thus reduce poverty in society and improve the lives of peoples in it.
